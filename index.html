Convienence and Efficiency

I belive learning piano should be fun without sacrificing convience. Up until recently having a personal piano instructor required spending countless time in traffic rushing to make your in-person lessons, often times missing out on precious minutes you had hopped to spend with your instructor. I bring a fun and efficient aproach to learning piano, utilizing elements of sightreading, improvising, and theory to have you playing songs you dreamed of within no time.

Tailored to You

Every student has a different learning style, with both strengths and weaknesses. I focus on helping take your natural talents and exemplifying them. Some students have an amazing sense of rythmn, some a great ear, some can connect the theory very well. With every lesson tailored specificly to you the lessons will be not only engaging but will propell you towards your goals!

Pricing
